Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how APP Tracker MD ("we", "our", "the platform") handles your information. APP Tracker MD is operated by Menzi Dlamini, an independent developer based in Africa.

1. Data We Collect

APP Tracker MD stores the following data exclusively in your browser's localStorage on your own device:

• Account credentials (name, email, encrypted password)
• App portfolio data (app names, package IDs, versions, links)
• User count analytics you manually enter
• API keys for cloud storage (stored locally only)
• Coins balance and reward history
• Login streak and achievement data

None of this data is transmitted to any server operated by APP Tracker MD. We have no backend database.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit:

• Your real name or identity beyond what you choose to enter
• Your IP address or location
• Your browsing history
• Any payment information

3. Google AdSense & Advertising

This site uses Google AdSense (Publisher ID: ca-pub-8607735774746684) to display advertisements. Google and its partners may use cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies to serve personalized advertisements based on your browsing history.

To opt out of personalized advertising: google.com/settings/ads

Google Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

4. Cookies

APP Tracker MD itself does not set first-party cookies. Third-party cookies may be set by Google AdSense for advertising purposes. You can control cookie preferences through our consent banner or your browser settings.

5. Cloud Storage (Optional)

If you configure cloud storage (Cloudflare R2, Backblaze B2, Amazon S3), your app files are uploaded directly from your browser to your own cloud bucket. APP Tracker MD never handles, receives, or stores these files. Your cloud credentials are stored only in your browser's localStorage.

6. GDPR Rights (EU/EEA Users)

If you are in the EU or EEA, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. Since all data is in your browser, you can exercise these rights by clearing your browser's localStorage for this site. For Google AdSense data, contact Google directly.

7. CCPA Rights (California Users)

California residents have the right to know what data is collected and to request deletion. We do not sell personal data. For Google's data practices, see Google's Privacy Policy.

8. Children's Privacy

APP Tracker MD is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
